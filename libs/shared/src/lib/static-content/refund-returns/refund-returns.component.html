Refund & Returns

  • Items must be returned within 30 days of delivery.
  • Products must be unused, unwashed, and in original packaging.
  • Sale or clearance items may not be eligible for return.

Submit a return request at support@fadedchapter.com.

Receive your return shipping label (if applicable).

Securely pack the item and send it back.

Once received, we process your refund within 5-7 business days.

  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.
  • It may take 5-10 business days for the refund to reflect.
  • Shipping costs are non-refundable.

We replace items only if they are defective or damaged. Contact us at support@fadedchapter.com.
