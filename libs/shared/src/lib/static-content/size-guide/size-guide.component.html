How to Measure:

Use a measuring tape and follow these guidelines to determine your size:

  • Chest/Bust: Measure around the fullest part, keeping the tape parallel to the floor.
  • Waist: Measure around the narrowest part of your waist.
  • Hips: Measure around the widest part of your hips.

Tip: If you're between sizes, choose the larger size for a more comfortable fit.
